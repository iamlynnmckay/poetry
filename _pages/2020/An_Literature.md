My Sundays hands flip through catalogues \
Of kitchens, pots, pans; \
Even here it sells, \
Pretty young thing, \
Faced of restraint, \
The mile a muzzle, suggestion \
Of giggles, \
Wares presented, \
Sex and toasters, \
Unkind art.
